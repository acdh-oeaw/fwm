oeai.METRIX

Your Gateway to Archaeometric Data

oeai.METRIX provides a structured and accessible platform for the documentation, analysis, and dissemination of archaeometric research data. The primary goals of oeai.METRIX are to support interdisciplinary archaeological science and to offer accurate, reliable datasets derived from a variety of analytical methods, including materials characterization and provenance studies. By employing controlled vocabularies and integrating visual resources from oeai.DAM, we ensure that the data is standardized, interoperable, and readily available for further research and comparative analysis.
